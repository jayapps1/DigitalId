About Digital ID

Digital ID is an internal, secure identity management and verification platform designed exclusively for authorized officers and administrators. It ensures accountability, data integrity, and trusted identity operations across all participating units.

High-Security Architecture

Built with strong encryption, access control, and audit trails to protect sensitive officer and identity records.

Controlled Officer Registration

Officers cannot self-register. All registrations and imports are handled by Station Administrators to maintain system trust.

Centralized Identity Management

Enables administrators to manage officers, verify credentials, approve requests, and monitor activities from one platform.

Real-Time Notifications

Officers and administrators receive instant alerts for approvals, requests, and system events to ensure timely action.

Role-Based Access Control

Access to features is strictly controlled based on user roles, ensuring officers only see what they are authorized to access.

Officer accounts are created and managed by Station Administrators. If you require access or updates, please contact your Station Administrator.
